NEURAL RESET PRO

Guidance & Protocols

Select a modality and adjust parameters in real time to match your neuro-auditory profile.

  • Notched Noise: Habituation therapy. Match the frequency to your tinnitus.
  • CR Neuromodulation: Randomized tones near your pitch to desynchronize neurons.
  • Binaural: Stress reduction via alpha wave induction.
  • Pure Vestibular (100Hz): Precision stimulus for motion sickness based on Nagoya University research[cite: 8, 21].

Instructions: For 100Hz therapy, listen for 60 to 90 seconds before travel[cite: 19, 87, 119]. Set volume between 80 to 85 dB for maximum efficacy[cite: 23, 31, 123].
